# Member Guide

This guide will help you navigate the Refugio reservation system to check availability, make reservations, and manage your bookings.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the login page">
    Open `login.php` in your web browser
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    - **Email**: Your registered email address
    - **Password**: Your password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Click "Acceder" to log in. You'll be redirected to your member dashboard (`viewSocio.php`)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you don't have an account, contact the administrator to create one for you with your member details.
</Note>

### Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see the main navigation menu:

- **Calendario**: View bed availability calendar
- **Nueva Reserva**: Create a new reservation
- **Mis Reservas**: View and manage your reservations
- **Mi Perfil**: Update your profile information

## Checking Availability

The calendar view helps you find available dates before making a reservation.

### Understanding the Calendar

Access the calendar by clicking **Calendario** in the sidebar.

**Color coding**:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Green" icon="check-circle">
    Many beds available (5 or more free beds)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Yellow" icon="exclamation-triangle">
    Few beds available (fewer than 5 free beds)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Red" icon="times-circle">
    No beds available (fully booked)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blue (solid)" icon="calendar-check">
    You have an approved reservation on this day
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cyan (dashed border)" icon="clock">
    You have a pending reservation on this day
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gray" icon="ban">
    Past date (cannot book)
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Reading Day Information

Each calendar day displays:
- **Day number** at the top
- **Available beds** (e.g., "15/26 libres" means 15 beds available out of 26 total)
- **Number of reservations** for that day
- **Your reservation details** if you have one

### Navigating Months

Use the left and right arrow buttons in the calendar header to move between months:
- **← (Anterior)**: View previous month
- **→ (Siguiente)**: View next month

The current month and year are displayed in the center.

### Quick Reservation from Calendar

Click any available day (green or yellow) to jump directly to the reservation form with that date pre-filled.

## Making a Reservation

Create a new reservation by clicking **Nueva Reserva** in the sidebar.

### Step 1: Select Dates and Room

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose check-in date">
    Click the "Fecha de Entrada" field to open the date picker. Select your arrival date.
    
    <Note>
      Dates marked in red are fully booked. You can still select them, but availability will be checked when you choose a room.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose check-out date">
    Click the "Fecha de Salida" field and select your departure date. It must be after the check-in date.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a room">
    After choosing dates, the room dropdown will populate with available rooms showing:
    - Room number
    - Number of beds available in that room
    
    Example: "Habitación 1 (4 camas disponibles)"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose number of beds">
    Use the + and - buttons to select how many beds you need (minimum 1)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You can only select as many beds as are available in your chosen room for your selected dates.
</Warning>

### Step 2: Describe Your Activity

In the "Actividad a Realizar" section, describe what activity you'll be doing during your stay:

**Examples**:
- "Hiking to Pico de la Sierra"
- "Mountain biking trails"
- "Rock climbing"
- "Photography expedition"

This helps the refuge manage activities and group similar interests.

### Step 3: Add Companions

<Note>
  If you're reserving more than 1 bed, you must add companion information for each additional bed.
</Note>

For example, if you reserve 3 beds, you need to add information for 2 companions (you are the first person).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Agregar Acompañante'">
    The button appears when you select more than 1 bed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in companion details">
    For each companion, enter:
    - **Es socio**: Select "Sí" if they're a club member, "No" if not
    - **Nº Socio**: Member number (only if they're a member)
    - **DNI**: National ID number (required)
    - **Nombre**: First name (required)
    - **Apellido 1**: First surname (required)
    - **Apellido 2**: Second surname (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Repeat for each companion">
    Add one companion entry for each additional bed beyond the first
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must add exactly the required number of companions. For example, 3 beds = 2 companions. The form will not submit if this doesn't match.
</Warning>

### Step 4: Add Comments (Optional)

Use the "Comentarios" field to:
- Request specific bed numbers or room locations
- Mention dietary requirements
- Note any special needs
- Provide additional information for administrators

### Step 5: Submit Your Reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your information">
    Double-check all dates, room selection, companions, and details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Crear Reserva'">
    Submit your reservation request
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    You'll see a success message: "Reserva creada exitosamente. Pendiente de aprobación por el administrador."
    
    Your reservation is now **pending** and awaits administrator approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Reservations

View and manage all your reservations by clicking **Mis Reservas** in the sidebar.

### Reservation Status

Your reservations are organized into three sections:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pending">
    **Pendientes de Aprobación** - Reservations awaiting administrator approval
    
    - Shows as cyan/dashed on calendar
    - Can be edited or canceled
    - Waiting for admin to approve or reject
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Approved">
    **Reservas Aprobadas** - Confirmed reservations
    
    - Shows as blue on calendar
    - Can be canceled (annulled)
    - Can be edited if the check-in date hasn't passed
    - These are your confirmed bookings
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Canceled">
    **Reservas Canceladas** - Rejected or canceled reservations
    
    - Past reservations that were canceled
    - Cannot be modified
    - Kept for your records
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Viewing Reservation Details

Each reservation shows:
- **Habitación**: Room number
- **Nº Camas**: Number of beds reserved
- **Fecha Entrada**: Check-in date (format: DD/MM/YYYY)
- **Fecha Salida**: Check-out date (format: DD/MM/YYYY)
- **Días**: Number of days (for approved reservations)
- **Solicitado**: When you made the request

### Editing a Pending Reservation

You can modify reservations that are still pending approval:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the reservation">
    Find it in the "Pendientes de Aprobación" section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Editar'">
    Click the blue "Editar" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Modify:
    - Check-in date
    - Check-out date
    - Room selection
    - Number of beds
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Submit the form to update your reservation
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Editing a pending reservation keeps it in pending status. It will still need administrator approval.
</Note>

### Editing an Approved Reservation

For approved reservations that haven't started yet:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the reservation">
    Look in the "Reservas Aprobadas" section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check if editable">
    The "Editar" button appears only if the check-in date is in the future
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Make changes">
    Update dates, room, or number of beds as needed
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit">
    Save your changes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  - You cannot edit reservations where the check-in date has passed
  - Changes to approved reservations are subject to availability
  - If beds aren't available, you'll see an error message
</Warning>

### Canceling a Pending Reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the pending reservation">
    Find it in the "Pendientes" section
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Cancelar'">
    Click the red "Cancelar" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm cancellation">
    Confirm in the dialog that appears
  </Step>
</Steps>

The reservation moves to "Canceladas" and beds are released.

### Annulling an Approved Reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the approved reservation">
    Locate it in "Reservas Aprobadas"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Anular'">
    Click the red "Anular" button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    A special confirmation dialog will appear warning you that this action cannot be undone
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Annulling an approved reservation is **permanent and cannot be undone**. If you change your mind, you'll need to create a new reservation.
</Warning>

## Managing Your Profile

Update your contact information by clicking **Mi Perfil** in the sidebar.

### Viewing Your Information

Your profile displays:
- Member number
- Full name
- DNI
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile photo (if uploaded)

### Updating Contact Information

You can update your:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Email address">
    Enter your new email in the email field
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Phone number">
    Update your phone number
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Click "Guardar Cambios" to update your profile
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your name, DNI, and member number cannot be changed by members. Contact an administrator if these need to be updated.
</Note>

### Uploading a Profile Photo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Cambiar Foto'">
    In the profile section, click the upload button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select your photo">
    Choose an image file:
    - Formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, or GIF
    - Maximum size: 5 MB
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload">
    The photo will be uploaded and displayed in your profile and sidebar
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Changing Your Password

To change your password:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Current password">
    Enter your current password for verification
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="New password">
    Enter your new password (minimum 6 characters recommended)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Re-enter your new password to confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Cambiar Contraseña"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  After changing your password, you'll need to use the new password for future logins.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot find available beds

**Problem**: All days show red or very few beds available

**Solutions**:
- Try different dates or time periods
- Check if there's a special event reserving the entire refuge
- Consider reserving fewer beds
- Contact the administrator for assistance

### Reservation not approved

**Problem**: Your reservation stays pending for a long time

**Possible reasons**:
- Administrator hasn't reviewed it yet (check back daily)
- There may be a conflict with another reservation
- Your request might need clarification

**Solution**: Contact the administrator if it's urgent or has been pending for several days.

### Cannot edit or cancel reservation

**Problem**: Edit or cancel buttons are disabled

**Reasons**:
- The check-in date has already passed (approved reservations)
- The reservation is already canceled
- You're looking at the wrong section

**Solution**: Pending reservations can always be edited or canceled. For approved reservations, the check-in date must be in the future.

### Form won't submit

**Problem**: "Crear Reserva" button doesn't work

**Common causes**:
- Number of companions doesn't match beds (e.g., 3 beds needs 2 companions)
- Required fields are empty
- Check-in date is after check-out date
- No room selected

**Solution**: Review all fields and ensure companion count matches bed count minus one.
